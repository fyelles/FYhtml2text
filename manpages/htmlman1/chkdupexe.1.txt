Name
chkdupexe — find duplicate executables

Synopsis

chkdupexe

 
DESCRIPTION
chkdupexe
      will scan the union of $PATH and a hardcoded list of common
      locations for binaries. It will report dangling symlinks and
      duplicately-named binaries.
