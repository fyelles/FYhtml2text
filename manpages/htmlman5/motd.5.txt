Section 5
 

 
 

Name
motd — message of the day

 
DESCRIPTION
The contents of /etc/motd
      are displayed by login(1) after a successful
      login but just before it executes the login shell.
The abbreviation "motd" stands for "message of the day",
      and this file has been traditionally used for exactly that
      (it requires much less disk space than mail to all
      users).

 
FILES
/etc/motd

 
SEE ALSO
login(1), issue(5)
